BGP Workshop

Tasks

  • setup VLANs for upstreams and IXes
  • peer with both Upstreams (IPv4 & IPv6)
    • create filter for your ranges
  • peer with both IX (IPv4 & IPv6)
  • create filter for only your ranges
  • peer with your immediate neighboors on the switch
  • configure upstream communities
  • configure downstream communities

We will provide you with example configurations for Bird and Quagga

  • Bird
  • Quagga

Each participant will receive a list with the following information:

  • Switch port number
  • VLAN to upstream1
  • VLAN to upstream2
  • VLAN to IX-1
  • VLAN to IX-2
  • VLAN to peer1
  • VLAN to peer2
  • Router ID
  • Public IP v4 & v6 range for upstreams
  • Private IP v4 & v6 range for IXes
